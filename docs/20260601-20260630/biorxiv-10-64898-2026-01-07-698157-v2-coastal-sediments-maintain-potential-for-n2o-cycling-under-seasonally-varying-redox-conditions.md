## 摘要
沿海富营养化可导致沉积物脱氧和硫化物积累，这可能强烈影响强效温室气体氧化亚氮(N2O)的动态。本研究调查了季节性静海相(缺氧和含硫化物)沿海盆地表层沉积物中氧(O2)和硫化物对微生物N2O产生和消耗的影响。春季，这些表层沉积物处于含氧状态，而在夏季水体分层期间则高度含硫化物。在春季含氧条件下，尽管批次培养中观察到硝化作用及通过不完全反硝化产生N2O的潜力，但表层沉积物中底物O2和硝酸盐(NO3-)的快速消耗限制了净原位N2O产生。基于宏基因组和宏转录组分析，消耗N2O的微生物群落表现出高度多样性，并由携带nosZ II类基因的黄杆菌纲主导。通过批次培养评估夏季含硫化物条件的表层沉积物，我们发现中等硫化物浓度(0.2-1 mM)增强了N2O消耗，而高浓度(4 mM)抑制了反硝化的所有步骤。这些发现突显了氧化还原对N2O动力学的控制，表明海岸沉积物即使在底物限制和硫化物浓度升高的情况下，仍能保持显著的N2O周转潜力。因此，改变O2、NO3-和硫化物可用性的生态系统恢复策略可能从根本上影响沿海N2O收支。

## Abstract
Coastal eutrophication can lead to deoxygenation and sulfide accumulation in sediments, which could strongly impact the dynamics of the potent greenhouse gas nitrous oxide (N2O). Here, we investigated the effects of oxygen (O2) and sulfide on microbial N2O production and consumption in surface sediments of a seasonally euxinic (anoxic and sulfidic) coastal basin. In spring, these surface sediments are oxygenated, while they are highly sulfidic during stratification of the water column in summer. During oxygenated spring conditions, rapid depletion of the substrates O2 and nitrate (NO3-) in the surface sediment limited net in situ N2O production, despite potential for nitrification and for N2O production through incomplete denitrification as observed in batch incubations. Based on metagenome and metatranscriptome analyses the N2O-consuming microbial community was shown to be highly diverse and dominated by clade II nosZ-possessing Flavobacteriia. Assessing the summer sulfidic conditions in surface sediments via batch incubations, we found that moderate sulfide concentrations (0.2-1 mM) enhanced N2O consumption, whereas high concentrations (4 mM) inhibited all steps of denitrification. These findings highlight the redox controls on N2O dynamics, by indicating that coastal sediments can maintain significant N2O turnover potential despite substrate limitations and elevated sulfide concentrations. Consequently, ecosystem restoration strategies that alter O2, NO3- and sulfide availability may fundamentally impact coastal N2O budgets.
